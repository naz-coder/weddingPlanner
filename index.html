The main objective for developing this project was to automate the operations of the wedding. There are are lots of operations and functionality done in wedding planner system, so I developed this project to automate all the functionality and maintain the data accuracy.

  • The system automates the manual procedure of managing wedding activities.
  • Customer can view their wedding planning records and details easily.
  • The system is convenient and flexible to be used.
  • It saves their time, efforts, money and resources.
  • The user has to manually keep updating the information.

A wedding planner is a professional who assists with the design, plannning and nanagement of client's wedding. Weddings are significant events in peopl's lives and as suchm couples are often willing to spend considerable amount of money to ensure that their weddings are well-organized. When a wedding planner first meets with a client, the conversation should consist of simply explaining the packages available and briefly discussing the type of wedding the client wants. Typically, a wedding planner is hired for either Full Service or Day-off Coordibation, and it is imperative that the planner fully explains to the client what is included in each of their packages.
